Make Your Own Website!

Making a website is not hard, sure you can get into complex fancy features, but the basics? Anyone can do them.
Not only can anyway make a website, but everyone should! It doesn't need a topic, you can just make a scrapbook of your daily life.

Reasons to make a website:

  1. You'll learn about how the web works, its not magic, you can do it!
  2. You'll have made something thats truly yours, no 2 websites have to look the same if you don't want them to.
  3. You'll have something that will last, when a social network closes everything on it is lost, but a static website can be saved forever.

How do I get my site online!?

If you want other people to be able to browse your site, you'll need to upload it to the internet.
For that you'll need to put it on a server, there are many companies that offer servers, here are some good indie ones.

  • Neocities - Big community, free ($5/m for extra features), very easy for beginners.
  • Ichi City - Tiny community, 100% free, easy for beginners.
  • Tilde Town - Small community, 100% free, more complex and geeky.
  • Tilde Club - Medium community, 100% free, more complex and geeky.
  • Leprd - No community, 100% free, big computing power for complex sites.
